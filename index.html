Questions

Frequently asked questions

Price Lock Promise

Will my weekly rent increase?

Once your cabin has been delivered and your rental agreement begins, your weekly rental price is locked in for as long as you continue renting that cabin. The weekly price you agree to at the start of your rental remains your price for the life of your agreement.

How much does a rental cabin cost?

Standard Cabin $80 per week. Extra Standard Cabin $110 per week. 2 weeks Bond. First week's rent paid in advance. Free delivery*

Do you deliver?

Yes, delivery is available from our Dargaville and Warkworth branches. We offer free local delivery throughout much of Northland and Auckland. Delivery outside our standard service area may incur additional charges.

What do I need on my property?

You will need suitable access for delivery and a reasonably level area for placement. We can confirm details when you enquire.

What can I use my cabin for?

Whether you want to work, rest, play, or earn, a RentCabins portable cabin gives you the freedom to create the space you need. Set up a quiet home office, a warm spare bedroom, a games room, hobby studio, home gym, beauty or massage room, or a dedicated business workspace. Looking for extra income? Many customers use our cabins as accommodation for family, long-term tenants, or short-term holiday stays such as Airbnb-style rentals, where permitted. It's an affordable way to unlock the potential of your property and create space that works for your lifestyle—and your future.
