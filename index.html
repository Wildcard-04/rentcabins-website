Questions

Frequently asked questions

How much does a cabin cost?

Hire starts from $80 per week. Final price may depend on delivery area, cabin type, and hire terms.

Do you deliver?

Yes, delivery is available from our Warkworth branch. Free local delivery may apply for qualifying hires.

What do I need on my property?

You will need suitable access for delivery and a reasonably level area for placement. We can confirm details when you enquire.

Can I use a cabin as an office?

Yes. Many people use portable cabins as a quiet work-from-home or admin space.
